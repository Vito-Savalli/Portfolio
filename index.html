Statify.

Statify is a web application using the power of API to collect and display data
from the top 100 tennis players.

Users can query Statify to access these data with a combination of
filters and preferences of their choice.

Then, data are rendered in the browser in real time using REACT.

Ever wonder what would be the outcome of betting
the victory of Nadal on his ten last games on clay ?

Just ask Statify !
